Plot Overview

Breaking Bad tells the story of Walter White, a struggling high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer. He turns to a life of crime, producing and selling methamphetamine with his former student Jesse Pinkman, with the goal of securing his family's financial future before he dies. The series tracks the moral decline of Walter as he transforms from a mild-mannered family man into a ruthless drug lord.
